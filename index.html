About the Firm

One Firm. Full Dedication. Real Results.

Accounting Tax Service is a solo CPA practice based in Tuguegarao City, Cagayan, founded on the belief that every business — no matter how small — deserves expert financial guidance and airtight tax compliance.

We provide personalized, hands-on accounting and tax services tailored to the specific needs of Filipino businesses. Whether you're a sari-sari store owner filing your first tax return or a growing corporation needing structured bookkeeping, we have you covered.

  • Licensed and regulated by the Professional Regulation Commission (PRC)
  • In-depth knowledge of BIR regulations, TRAIN Law, and Philippine GAAP
  • Serving clients in Cagayan Valley and beyond via remote engagement
  • Confidential, trustworthy, and responsive client service
  • Affordable professional rates designed for SMEs and startups
What We Offer

Comprehensive Accounting & Tax Services

From day-to-day bookkeeping to complex tax planning, we provide a complete suite of financial services to keep your business compliant and thriving.

📒

Bookkeeping Services

Accurate, organized financial records maintained in accordance with Philippine accounting standards — the backbone of every healthy business.

  • General ledger maintenance
  • Accounts payable & receivable management
  • Bank reconciliation
  • Subsidiary ledger upkeep
  • Monthly financial reports
🧾

BIR Tax Filing & Compliance

Complete preparation and filing of all BIR returns to keep your business penalty-free and compliant with the latest TRAIN Law provisions.

  • Income Tax Returns (1701, 1702, 1701A)
  • VAT Returns (2550M, 2550Q)
  • Percentage Tax (2551Q)
  • Withholding Tax (1601C, 1601EQ, 1604CF)
  • Annual registration renewal (0605)
📈

Financial Statement Preparation

Professionally prepared financial statements compliant with Philippine Financial Reporting Standards (PFRS/PAS), ready for BIR, SEC, or bank submission.

  • Balance sheet & income statement
  • Statement of cash flows
  • Notes to financial statements
  • Comparative financial analysis
  • Management reports
🏢

Business Registration & Compliance

End-to-end assistance in setting up and maintaining your business registrations with all relevant government agencies.

  • BIR registration & TIN application
  • SEC incorporation assistance
  • DTI business name registration
  • LGU/Mayor's permit guidance
  • Books of accounts registration
🔍

Tax Audit Assistance & Representation

Expert representation and preparation when the BIR comes knocking — protecting your rights and minimizing exposure during audits and assessments.

  • Letter of Authority (LOA) response
  • BIR audit documentation preparation
  • Tax assessment evaluation
  • Compromise settlement assistance
  • Protest letter preparation
💡

Tax Planning & Consulting

Proactive strategies to legally minimize your tax burden while maximizing compliance — smart planning before the filing deadline arrives.

  • Tax exposure review & assessment
  • Optional Standard Deduction (OSD) analysis
  • Itemized vs. OSD comparison
  • Business structure advisory
  • Year-end tax planning strategies
💼

Payroll Services

Accurate and timely payroll processing ensuring compliance with BIR, SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG requirements for your employees.

  • Monthly payroll computation
  • Payslip preparation
  • 13th month pay & separation pay
  • SSS, PhilHealth, Pag-IBIG remittances
  • Alpha list & BIR 2316 preparation
🌐

Management & Advisory Services

Beyond compliance — strategic financial insights to help your business grow, plan, and make informed decisions with confidence.

  • Cash flow forecasting
  • Budget preparation & monitoring
  • Business profitability analysis
  • Break-even & cost analysis
  • Financial health diagnostics

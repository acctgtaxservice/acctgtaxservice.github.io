About the Template

Built for Philippine Payroll Compliance

Stop doing payroll from scratch every month. This Excel template handles all the computations automatically — from gross pay to statutory deductions — so you can process payroll in minutes, not hours.

Designed by licensed CPAs with deep knowledge of BIR TRAIN Law, SSS, PhilHealth, and Pag-IBIG regulations effective 2026.

  • Auto-computed SSS, PhilHealth & Pag-IBIG contributions
  • BIR withholding tax table (TRAIN Law 2026 rates)
  • 13th month pay & holiday pay calculator
  • Works on Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets
  • No macros required — fully formula-based
  • Printable payslip layout included
